Signature Services

Homegrown in the Twin Lakes Area

Twin Lakes Epoxy is based right here in Mountain Home, Arkansas — not a call center, not a national franchise. When you reach out, you're talking to the people who show up on your job site, run the grinder, and stand behind the finished floor.

We took the name "Twin Lakes" seriously: our service area covers the Ozark foothills between our two namesake lakes — Norfork Lake and Bull Shoals Lake. Wherever you're located within 75 miles of Mountain Home, we'll come to you.

What We Specialize In Epoxy counter tops, epoxy garage floors, and enclosed trailer floor coatings are our bread and butter — but we also install basement floors, metallic epoxy, patio coatings, commercial systems, and more. If it's concrete or it needs a durable coating, we've got a system for it.

Why We Do It This Way

Too many floor coatings fail within a few years because installers skip the boring but critical step: surface preparation. We diamond grind every surface before coating — no acid etching, no shortcuts — because a coating is only as good as what it's bonded to.

  • Full diamond grinding surface prep on every job
  • Professional-grade polyaspartic/polyurea hybrid coatings
  • Durable finish that resists fading, peeling, and yellowing
  • Most projects completed start to finish in a single day
  • Honest, no-pressure free estimates
