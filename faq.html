Garage Floors & Coatings

How long does an epoxy garage floor last?▾
With professional-grade polyaspartic coatings and full diamond grinding prep, expect 15–25 years of durability, backed by our written lifetime warranty.
How long before I can drive on my new floor?▾
Foot traffic within hours, vehicle traffic within 24 hours thanks to our fast-curing polyaspartic system.
Why do some epoxy floors peel and fail?▾
The #1 cause is skipped or inadequate surface preparation. We use full diamond grinding on every job — no acid etching, no shortcuts.
Can you install coatings in winter?▾
Yes, with the right conditions. Concrete surface temperature must meet a minimum threshold — we evaluate this on every project and advise accordingly.

Counter Tops

Is epoxy safe for kitchen countertops?▾
Yes — once cured, our topcoat is food-safe, non-porous, and easy to sanitize.
Can you coat over existing counters?▾
In most cases yes, avoiding the cost and mess of a full tear-out.

Enclosed Trailers

Can you coat a trailer with a wood deck?▾
Yes — wood decking is the most common surface we coat, using a moisture-blocking primer suited to plywood.
How long is my trailer out of service?▾
Most trailer floor coatings are completed in 1–2 days, usable again within 24–48 hours.

Warranty & Payment
